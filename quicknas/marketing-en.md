# QuickNAS Marketing

Last updated: February 14, 2026

QuickNAS is a macOS menu bar app that makes uploading files to Synology NAS fast and predictable.

## 1. Product Positioning

- Platform: macOS
- Product type: Menu bar utility for Synology NAS uploads
- Core promise: Move files to NAS in fewer steps, with clearer transfer control

## 2. Core Value

- Upload from a lightweight menu bar workflow
- Sign in with Direct URL or QuickConnect
- Choose direct upload or Upload and Share
- Control duplicate file behavior (overwrite or skip)
- Keep transfer history and status visible in one place

## 3. Feature Highlights

### A. Fast Access

- Menu bar-first interaction for quick, repeated uploads
- Streamlined path and account selection

### B. Flexible NAS Connection

- Supports Direct URL and QuickConnect login methods
- Session-based workflow designed for practical daily use

### C. Transfer Control

- Duplicate-file handling options
- Multi-file/folder upload packaging in ZIP
- Package size control (up to 4 GB)

### D. Support-Ready Diagnostics

- In-app bug reporting flow
- Optional support package with recent diagnostic logs for faster triage

## 4. Typical Use Cases

- Creative teams moving drafts and exports to NAS
- Home lab users centralizing files from macOS
- Ops/admin workflows that require frequent upload-and-share tasks
- Anyone replacing repeated browser upload steps with a native workflow

## 5. App Store Connect Copy (English)

### Promotional Text

Upload to Synology NAS from your Mac menu bar with direct upload or upload-and-share workflows, transfer controls, and practical session management.

### Subtitle Candidates

### Keyword Candidates

synology,nas,upload,file transfer,quickconnect,menu bar,macos,share,zip

### Short Description

QuickNAS helps you upload files to Synology NAS from a native macOS menu bar experience with direct upload, upload-and-share, and transfer controls.
